Gazpacho

A Spanish cold soup, perfect for the summer.

    Ingredients
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Bread
    Steps
  1. Chop the vegetables and the garlic
  2. Mix the vegetables in a bowl
  3. Add the bread and the liquids
  4. Let it chill for at least 2 hours
  5. Put everything in the blender and serve
